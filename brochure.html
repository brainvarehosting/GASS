From the Founder

A note on why we do this work.

Across nearly three decades of working alongside boards, founders, and institutions, one observation has stayed with me: the gap between a good idea and a lasting outcome is rarely closed by strategy alone. It is closed by clarity of thought, quality of decisions, and the discipline to execute through difficulty.

GreenApple Success Factors exists to help leaders close that gap. Whether it is a family-led enterprise charting succession, a first-time founder navigating early scale, or an institution rethinking its place in a changing world — our role is to bring perspective, structure, and a steady partner through critical inflection points.

We treat every engagement as a relationship rather than a transaction. We listen first, challenge respectfully, and stay close to the work until results are visible and durable. The pages that follow set out our practices and philosophy — but the heart of what we do is always the same: to enable people and organisations to think beyond the obvious, act with clarity, and create lasting impact.

Dr. George V Antony
FOUNDER · GREENAPPLE SUCCESS FACTORS PVT LTD

CHAPTER 04 · ABOUT

A consulting and mentoring practice built around decision-ready intelligence and disciplined execution.

GreenApple Success Factors Pvt Ltd was founded with a clear purpose — to bridge the gap between strategy and execution, vision and result. We partner with boards, entrepreneurs and academic institutions to shape future-ready organisations through strategic insight, decision intelligence, and empowered leadership.

With over two decades of combined experience spanning industry, academia, research, and consulting, our team brings a distinctive blend of strategic foresight and execution excellence. This enables us to partner closely with organisations in achieving their goals with clarity, precision, and timeliness.

Our approach is client-centric and outcome-focused, ensuring that every engagement delivers measurable and meaningful impact. We emphasise deep collaboration, a strong understanding of business dynamics, and a disciplined focus on translating strategy into tangible results.

Our advisory extends to financial growth and wealth management — supporting individuals and organisations in wealth creation, asset optimisation, corporate funding, and long-term financial stability.

The bridge between strategy and execution is built one clear decision at a time.
