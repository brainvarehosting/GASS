From the Founder

A note on why we do this work.

Across nearly three decades of working alongside boards, founders, and institutions, one observation has stayed with me: the gap between a good idea and a lasting outcome is rarely closed by strategy alone. It is closed by clarity of thought, quality of decisions, and the discipline to execute through difficulty.

GreenApple Success Factors exists to help leaders close that gap. Whether it is a family-led enterprise charting succession, a first-time founder navigating early scale, or an institution rethinking its place in a changing world — our role is to bring perspective, structure, and a steady partner through critical inflection points.

We treat every engagement as a relationship rather than a transaction. We listen first, challenge respectfully, and stay close to the work until results are visible and durable.

Dr. George V Antony
Founder · GreenApple Success Factors Pvt Ltd
