About Us

Enabling Leadership to Think Beyond the Obvious

At GreenApple Success Factors Pvt Ltd, we believe that true success is not accidental — it is designed through clarity, shaped by insight, and sustained through disciplined execution.

GreenApple was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between strategy and execution, vision and result. We partner with boards, entrepreneurs, and institutions to shape future-ready organizations through strategic insight, decision intelligence, and empowered leadership.

With over two decades of combined experience spanning industry, academia, research, and consulting, our team brings a distinctive blend of strategic foresight and execution excellence.
