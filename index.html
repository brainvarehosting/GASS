About the Book

Officially released on June 28, 2025 by Union Minister Sushri Shobha Karandlaje, this handbook brings together decades of mentoring experience to help entrepreneurs and startup leaders navigate the realities of building enduring businesses in India.

What You'll Gain

  • Strategic wisdom drawn from real mentoring engagements
  • Understanding of the critical mistakes founders most often make
  • Frameworks for effective mentor–mentee collaboration
  • Tools for assessing and measuring a mentor's contribution

Ideal For

  • MSME business owners and startup founders
  • Practising and aspiring mentors
  • Academicians and institution leaders
